# Teacher English Lesson Plan

**Scenario:** Places I Can Go

**Theme:** I’m at School

- Objectives: identify places and make polite requests.
- Vocabulary: school, classroom, teacher, student, desk, chair.
- Functions: requests, directions.
- Grammar: Can/Can’t; There is/There are; prepositions.

## PPP Flow: TPR; model requests; role-play; exit ticket.
